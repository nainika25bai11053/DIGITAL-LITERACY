#  Prevention Checklist
##  Stay Safe Online 

Digital safety is a critical skill. Follow these essential guidelines to protect your personal and financial information:

###  Account & Password Security
* **Strong Passwords:** Use complex passwords and never reuse the same password across multiple accounts.
* **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Always enable 2FA for your email, social media, and banking applications.
* **Privacy of Credentials:** Never share your **OTP, PIN, or passwords** with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank.

### Browsing & App Safety
* **Phishing Awareness:** Avoid clicking on unknown links or suspicious messages to prevent phishing attacks.
* **Trusted Sources:** Download apps only from verified platforms like the **Google Play Store** or **Apple App Store**.
* **Secure Connections:** Always check website URLs for **HTTPS ** before entering any personal data.
* **Public Computers:** Always log out from public or shared computers immediately after use.
* **Updates:** Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.

### UPI & Financial Safety
* **Unknown Requests:** Never approve unknown payment requests on apps like **Google Pay** or **PhonePe**.
* **The Golden Rule:** Remember—you **only** need to enter your UPI PIN to **send** money, never to receive it.
